A L W A Y S


There’s still a lot He’s got to tell

But He just always wanted you to be well.

Every second, every minute, every hour of every day.

It has always been and will always be your name…

Written in His heart, tattooed on His mind.


He knows you needed to heal

To hopefully rekindle your love, so real.

“I love and miss you so” will always be the words He says.

It has always been and will always be your eyes…

Creating endless sparks within Him, looking through eternity with Him by your side.


Your happiness is what matters to Him most.

Every intricate thing about you is what He boasts.

Oh, how He wishes you call upon His name whenever you’re in need.

It has always been and will always be your smile…

Bringing joy into His life, painting colors ever since the day you believed in Him.
